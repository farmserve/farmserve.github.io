Welcome to Farmserve, your poultry partner

To ensure that our business is sustainable and profitable, it is very important to us that our end users also have lasting, profitable businesses.

To this end we are committed to training and educating our customers in the correct management procedures via our Poultry Workshops which are held at our premises in Umhlali KZN, every 3-4 months.We aim to supply good quality poultry feed and maize products at affordable prices and offer free advice to our customers. We also supply poultry equipment, medication and day-old chicks (to order).

We have our own vehicles and can transport up to 9 tons in a single delivery.
